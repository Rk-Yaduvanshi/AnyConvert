Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2024. Your privacy is at the heart of our mission.

1. Data Collection

At PDFFlow, we minimize the data we collect. We do not require account creation for basic conversion tasks. If you use our services, we may collect:

  • The files you upload (deleted instantly after processing).
  • Technical logs for debugging (IP addresses are anonymized).
  • Email addresses for account-based features.

2. How We Use Data

We use your data strictly to provide and improve the services you request. We never sell your personal information or the content of your documents to third parties. Document processing is automated and isolated from human oversight.

3. Security Standards

We employ enterprise-grade security protocols:

  • AES-256 encryption at rest.
  • TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit.
  • Automated deletion scripts that wipe temporary storage every hour.

4. Cookie Policy

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. Analytics cookies are only used with your explicit consent to help us understand how users navigate our site.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to access, delete, or port your data. You can request a complete data export from your account settings at any time.
